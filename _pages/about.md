Hi! I'm a first year grad student in the MIT Biology program. I study computational protein design with Professor [Amy Keating](https://www.keatinglab.mit.edu/). 

Previously I worked with Professor Steve Mayo at Caltech, designing peptide binders to viral targets. I have a great interest in structures, biophysics, and computational modeling. 

Beyond research, I'm committed to scientific outreach to the public and hope to engage further in scientific mentorship in graduate school. If I didn't love molecular biology so much, I like to think that I would have studied fish.
